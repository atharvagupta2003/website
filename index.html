From first “hello” to forever

Five introductions, not a thousand swipes. Five people worth actually meeting. The day you go exclusive is the day Coupld becomes something new: less about finding love, more about keeping it good.

01

A Matchmaker, not a filter

Height, distance, a few tick boxes, and here is everyone who is left. That is not matchmaking, that is a database query. Ours does the opposite: a conversation that picks up on the things filters miss, then introductions made with intention.

02

Just five matches worth meeting

One batch of five deeply compatible people, curated through ongoing profiling. No scrolling. No swiping. No fatigue. Every person in your stack was placed there for a reason, and your matchmaker can tell you exactly what that reason is.

03

Activate Signals when you are both out there

Switch on Signal and, only when you choose to, Coupld lets you know when someone you would click with is nearby and signalling too. No constant tracking, no being found when you would rather not be.

04

The app changes when you do

Go exclusive, and Coupld transitions with you. Matching switches off. Billing eases up, you are not dating anymore. Nothing you have built disappears. What is left is an app made for the relationship you are in, not the search for one.

05

Safety is a feature, not a community statement

Identity verification, catfish and scam detection, and safe check-ins are built in, not bolted on. Not buried in the terms. Safety is not a policy we wrote to cover ourselves. It is something you actually feel inside the product.
